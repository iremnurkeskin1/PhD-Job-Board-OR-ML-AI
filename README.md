# PhD Industry Job Board — OR × ML × AI

🔗 https://ph-d-job-board-or-ml-ai.vercel.app/

A job board for PhD-level industry roles in Operations Research, Machine Learning, and AI.

---

## Pages

### Job Board
Live job postings pulled from 40+ company career pages via public APIs. Select a category to search — results stream in as they are found.

**12 categories:**
OR Scientist / Engineer, OR PhD Internship, ML Research Scientist, ML Research Engineer, ML PhD Internship, AI Research Scientist, Reinforcement Learning, NLP / LLM Scientist, Quantitative Researcher, Quant PhD Internship, Applied Scientist, Data Scientist (Research)

**Features:**
- Filter by Full-Time or Internship
- Sort by relevance or date posted
- Export results as CSV
- Copy job link
- Interview info button on select companies (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google DeepMind, Jane Street, HRT, IMC, Optiver, Point72, Schonfeld, Jump Trading, DoorDash, Palantir, Databricks, Scale AI) — shows rounds, format, and what to expect

**Job sources:** Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, USAJobs.gov

---

### Recruiting Calendar
Recruiting timelines for 65+ companies showing when applications open, when interviews happen, and expected start dates. Grouped by sector with filters for sector and Full-Time vs Internship.

---

### Company Directory
200 companies with:
- Roles they hire PhDs for
- Full-time and internship timelines
- Visa sponsorship indicator
- Interview notes
- Direct link to their career page

Filterable by sector, role type, and visa sponsorship. Sectors: Big Tech, AI Lab, Quant Finance, Hedge Fund, Consulting, Logistics/OR, Finance/ML, Healthcare AI, Biotech, Semiconductor, Energy/OR, Defense, Government, Research Lab, Industry/OR.

---

### Resources
Curated books, courses, engineering articles, and interview prep by field. Fields: Operations Research, Machine Learning, Quant Finance, NLP/LLM, Reinforcement Learning. Each field has specialization filters.

---

## Data sources

All listings link directly to employer career pages. No scraping.

- Greenhouse API
- Lever API  
- Ashby API
- USAJobs.gov API
